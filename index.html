What we do

We run after school workshops for elementary school aged kids where we introduce them emerging technologies. Each workshop leads the children through the steps of the design thinking process.

We then take the lesson plans of these workshops and share it with the our community of education enthusiasts.

Parents, teachers, makers, educators can learn how to run these in their own local communities.

Our workshop materials are free even for commercial use. That means if you want to run a similar workshop in your school, make space or community center then you are welcome to do so! Of course we are happy to assist you along the way.

Online Courses

We provide online courses for parents, teachers, educators who want to teach children emerging technologies and are wondering how they can do that. We train the trainer.

You will also have plenty of opportunities to engage, share your experiences and help shape the course to capture the collective bets practices.

Among the course assets you will find the lesson plans and support materials that you need to organize your own workshops.

http://edx.warmersun.com
