Warmer Sun is transforming and augmenting education to prepare children for the future, knowing that the future is not what it used to be.

We pick emerging technologies that we know re going to skyrocket in the next decade and introduce them children in a fun, playful way.

Online Courses

We provide online courses for parents, teachers, educators who want to teach children emerging technologies and are wondering how they can do that. It's a sort of "train the trainer".

Open Edx is the same software platform that is powering Edx.org and is used by Harvard University, MIT, Stanford University and many others. Thanks to their effort we can build our online courses using the same technology.
