Warmer Sun is transforming and augmenting education to prepare children for the future, knowing that the future is not what it used to be.

We pick emerging technologies that we know are going to skyrocket in the next decade and introduce them children in a fun, playful way ...and you can do it too!

What we do

We create lesson plans for 2 hour workshops. Each workshop leads the children through the steps of the design thinking process.
They start with a short story that gets the kids' imagination moving and funnels their creativity into solving fictive ( or very real? ) problems.
To solve the problem they will use an emerging technology - such as 3D printing for instance - as a tool. The themes are different for each session and they always correspond to a real-world application of the technology.

We run these workshops in Vancouver, BC.

Parents, teachers, educators can learn how to run these in their local communities.

Our workshop materials are free for non-commercial use. If you are interested in commercial use then please contact us!
